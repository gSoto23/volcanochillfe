Amenities at VolcanoChill

Experience the best of Costa Rica's natural wonders and cultural treasures with the quality amenities available at VolcanoChill.

Proximity to Poás Volcano: Our mountain retreat is conveniently located near the majestic Poás Volcano, allowing you to easily explore one of Costa Rica's most iconic natural wonders.
Scenic Lookouts: Take in breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape from our on-site lookout points, perfect for capturing unforgettable moments and soaking in the tranquility of the mountains.
Local Restaurants: Indulge in delicious cuisine at nearby restaurants, where you can savor authentic Costa Rican flavors and culinary delights.
Hiking Trails: Embark on scenic hikes along well-marked trails, winding through lush forests and offering glimpses of stunning waterfalls and wildlife along the way.
Relaxation and Recreation: After a day of exploration, unwind at our property with amenities such as hammocks, outdoor seating areas, and a cozy campfire where you can share stories and stargaze with fellow travelers.

Poas Volcano

Located just 10 kilometers from Poás Volcano National Park, VolcanoChill offers unparalleled access to one of Costa Rica's most iconic natural wonders. Experience the awe-inspiring beauty of the volcano and its surroundings, knowing that your cozy mountain retreat is just a short distance away. Explore the park's trails, crater viewpoints, and diverse ecosystems, all within easy reach from VolcanoChill.
